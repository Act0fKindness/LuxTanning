# Policies

## Skin Sensitivity
- Always recommend customers perform a patch test on a small area before a full session.
- Advise customers with medical conditions to speak with a healthcare professional before tanning.

## Safety
- Goggles are mandatory for every session.
- Staff must approve kiosk sessions above 15 minutes.

## Payments
- Wallet top-ups and memberships are processed via Luma OS secure checkout.
- No cash is stored onsite; customers can use Apple Pay or card.

## Cancellations
- Missed appointments are non-refundable, but we can convert the missed minutes to wallet credit once per month.
